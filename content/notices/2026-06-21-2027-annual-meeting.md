Notice is hereby given that the **2027 Annual Meeting** of the Common & Recent Bogey Golf Club will be held on the **Summer Solstice — Monday, June 21, 2027**, at **Forest Dunes Golf Club, Roscommon, Michigan**. Time will be confirmed by the Captain in advance of the meeting.

## Anticipated Agenda

1. Call to order
2. Reading and approval of the minutes of the [2026 Annual Meeting](/minutes/minutes-2026-annual-meeting/)
3. Report of the Secretary-Treasurer
4. Election of officers
5. New business
6. Adjournment

This notice is given under [Article V, Section 1](/governance/bylaws/#article-v--meetings) of the Bylaws, which requires not less than fourteen (14) days' written notice to all Members.
