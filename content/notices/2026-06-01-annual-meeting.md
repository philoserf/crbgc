Notice is hereby given that the **2026 Annual Meeting** of the Common & Recent Bogey Golf Club will be held on **Monday, June 15, 2026 at 7:00 PM** by video conference.

## Agenda

1. Call to order
2. Reading and approval of minutes from the prior meeting
3. Report of the Secretary-Treasurer
4. Election of officers
5. New business
6. Adjournment

This notice is given under Article V, Section 1 of the Bylaws, which requires not less than fourteen (14) days' written notice to all Members.
