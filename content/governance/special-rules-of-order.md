Under [Article VI](/governance/bylaws/#article-vi--parliamentary-authority) of the Bylaws, _Robert's Rules of Order Newly Revised_ governs the Club in all cases not inconsistent with the Bylaws or Standing Rules. The Club may adopt Special Rules of Order to modify parliamentary procedure where _RONR_ defaults are inadequate.

**None have been adopted as of June 21, 2026.**
