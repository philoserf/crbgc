The 2026 **Dew Sweeper Championship** will be contested at sunrise on **Friday, August 7, 2026**, per the [Annual Traditions](/governance/standing-rules/#annual-traditions) section of the Standing Rules.

Members shall report to the first tee while the dew still holds the fairways. Under the [Dew Sweeper Priority](/governance/standing-rules/#rules-of-play) rule, the Member first off the tee shall hold bragging rights for the rest of that day.
